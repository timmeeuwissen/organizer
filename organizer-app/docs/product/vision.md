# Product Vision

## Overview

Organizer App is a comprehensive personal organization platform. It helps users manage behaviors, people, projects, tasks, meetings, mail, and coaching data in one place, with support for external integrations (Google, Microsoft) and AI capabilities.

## Non-Negotiable Goals

- **Single source of truth**: All personal organization data accessible from one application
- **Integration-first**: Connect to Gmail, Google Calendar, Google People, Exchange, Office 365 for calendars, contacts, tasks, and mail
- **AI-assisted**: Optional AI analysis and assistance (XAI, OpenAI, Gemini)
- **Privacy-aware**: User data stored in Firebase; OAuth used for external providers
- **Multi-language**: English and Dutch support
- **Offline-friendly**: Design for unreliable connectivity (per rules)

## Target Users

- Individuals managing personal and work life
- People who want behavior tracking, coaching notes, and actionable insights
- Users who rely on multiple productivity tools and want a unified view

## Core Value Proposition

Bring together behaviors, contacts, projects, tasks, meetings, mail, and coaching in one place, with optional sync to Google/Microsoft ecosystems and AI-powered analysis.
